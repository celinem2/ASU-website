About Us

The LIU African Student Union (ASU) is a community dedicated to celebrating African culture, heritage, and unity on campus. We provide a welcoming space for students to connect, share experiences, and engage in meaningful discussions. Whether you're looking to celebrate your roots, learn about African culture, or make new friends, ASU is the place for you!
